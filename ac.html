Assassins Creed

Assassins Creed is a fantastic tale about an assassin in the early 1900, hes trying to uncover the secrets that came of his parents death and track down the people responsible for it. The game has great customizable features such as the weapons your assassin carrys and how you would like him to look

The multiplayer for this game is also a masterpiece with diffrent players trying to hunt one another down in the untimate showdown.
